Lasagna

Classic Lasagna from the Old Country

This Lasagna will stop your heart in it's tracks. It's meat and fat and more gooey fat. What a way to go!

Pile on the pounds with this delicious throwback to ancient something or other.

Ingredients

  • 150g Cheddar Cheese
  • A knob of butter
  • 500g fatty mince
  • One large onion. chopped
  • Three cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 750ml jar of bechemel sauce
  • Pasta Sheets

Steps

  1. Gently fry the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the mince and brown.
  3. Chuck in the sauce and mix it all up.
  4. Layer the pasta sheets with the mince and sauce mix.
  5. Bake in an oven.
  6. Take out the oven and throw this in the bin.
  7. Go to an Italian restaurant.
